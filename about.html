My approach

I practice evidence-based psychiatry, always balancing the latest clinical guidelines with the unique needs and preferences of each patient. To me, the best treatment plans are ones that are not only clinically sound but also personally meaningful to the individual. I strive to create a space where patients feel heard, validated, and engaged in making informed choices about their care. I believe that quality mental health care requires respect, flexibility, and honesty. My role is not to dictate, but to guide and support. I approach care as a conversation, a negotiation, not a command. Gone are the days of paternalistic medicine. Instead, I work with my patients to build a shared understanding of their goals, challenges, and strengths, so we can move forward together.

Expectations for the first session

Your first session with me will be a thorough and thoughtful evaluation. We will discuss your history, current concerns, past treatments, and overall well-being, mental and physical. My goal is to fully understand your story before recommending any interventions, and to begin building a relationship based on trust and collaboration.
